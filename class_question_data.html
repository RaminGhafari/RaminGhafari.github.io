Zoodemic
Zoodemic is an engaging mobile game developed in partnership with the University of Nebraska Medical Center (UNMC), created to educate students on the dynamic and evolving field of viruses. Through interactive gameplay, Zoodemic offers a unique, immersive way to learn about infectious diseases and viral outbreaks.
